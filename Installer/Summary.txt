Game’s Name 
ROBOTEST
Genre
2.5D Platformer , Adventure , Puzzle, Sidescroller ,shooter
Description
A generation of robots that learn from the environment.  whose purpose is to work on environments where no man can access...
You will represent part of one robot’s specific algorithm by controlling it and learning to use the environment resources
